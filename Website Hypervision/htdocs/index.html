Entertainment and Gaming

VR provides an unparalleled level of immersion in gaming and entertainment experiences, allowing users to step into virtual worlds, interact with characters and objects, and participate in thrilling adventures.

Training and Simulation

VR offers a safe and cost-effective platform for training and simulation, allowing users to practice real-world scenarios in a controlled virtual environment, such as flight simulation, medical training, or industrial simulations.

Therapeutic and Rehabilitation Applications

VR has shown promise in therapeutic and rehabilitation settings, helping individuals with phobias, PTSD, or physical disabilities through exposure therapy and interactive rehabilitation exercises.

Real-world Overlay

AR overlays digital information onto the real world, enhancing the user’s perception and understanding of their environment.

Enhanced Visualization

AR enables users to visualize and comprehend complex data, models, or structures by overlaying virtual elements onto physical objects, enhancing understanding and decision-making.

FAQs

What is HyperVision?
HyperVision uses virsual and augmented reality to help people to learn and practice new skills. The app will provide one safe and believable virtual world where users will be able to interact with objects and information while learning new skills. In this way, the company will offer an innovative solution for the training and development of the skills of its users.
Do you need a account?
Yes you will need a HyperVision account. You can make one in the HyperVision site.
Where is the app for the HyperView VR and the HyperView AR?
There is an for the HyperView VR and the HyperView AR called ‘Vision’ which allows you to access a number of functions and is available for free in Apple/Google stores.
Can I use any HyperVision product Outdoor or Outside the House?
You only can use the HyperView AR. With the HyperView VR is not recommended since the direct sunlight can damage the lenses and affect the sensors. You can refer to the manual that comes with it.
Where can I get help?
You can go to the bottom of the site in the category Company and click the Contact for assistance. Make sure you are ready with information such as orders, accounts, devices etc.
Is HyperView VR and HyperView AR the same thing?
No, HyperView VR and HyperView AR are not the same thing, although they are related technologies.
Do you need a PC for the HyperView VR and the HyperView AR?
No there are standalone devices.
What else can you do with the HyperView VR and the HyperView AR?
The HyperView VR and the HyperView AR are primarily virtual reality devices, although they can do more. For example, you can use them to view movies or concerts, interact with friends socially, or even hold virtual work meetings and training sessions.
I have eyesight problems and wear glasses. Can I enjoy the HyperView VR?
Of course. When you buy the HyperView VR, it comes together with the glasses spacer that can help you wear your spectacles while using the headset. Besides, you can also consider in buying prescription lenses for greater viewing.
Can my kids use HyperVision products?
It is not recommended for kids or children below 13 years old. Devices are designed for ages 13 years and above. The reason is that the younger kids have greater risk of getting injured and experience adverse affects when using them.
